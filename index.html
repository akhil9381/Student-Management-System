Welcome to Student Management System

A Student Management System (SMS) is a comprehensive software platform designed to automate, streamline, and centralize all administrative and academic operations within an educational institution. By replacing traditional, disconnected paper files and spreadsheets with a unified cloud database, the system securely manages a student's entire lifecycle from initial admission and daily attendance tracking to grading, fee collection, and graduation. This digital hub ensures that sensitive academic and financial records are protected through role-based access control, allowing administrators to manage school workflows efficiently from a single dashboard.
